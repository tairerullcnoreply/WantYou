Three simple statuses

Every interaction in WantYou starts with a clear headline so both people know where they stand.

Know you

Let someone know you recognize them. Chats that begin here read “This user knows you”.

Want you

Share your interest silently. Until you reveal yourself, their chat shows “This user wants you”.

Both

Confirm the spark goes both ways. Conversations open with “This user both knows and wants you”.

Your interest, your rules

Keep a Want You anonymous for as long as you like. They can chat, get to know you, and request a reveal. You decide when to make it public.

  • Anonymous chats use private profiles and safe display names.
  • Revealing is a one-tap action—no pressure and no accidental reveals.
  • Switch to a regular chat at any time if you feel comfortable being known.
